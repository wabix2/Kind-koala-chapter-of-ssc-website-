About us

kindness with structure · empathy in action

Mission

Foster a culture of compassion through service, environmental care, and peer support – grounded in Ethiopian values of "መረብ" (community).

Values

Integrity, inclusive leadership, local action. We believe small gestures create lasting change.

Origin

Founded in 2023 by students who wanted a safe, kind space within SSC. Now an official chapter with school-wide reach.

We don't just meet – we build a kinder generation.
